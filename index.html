About

Built on public data. Delivered with precision.

Prospect Signal AU builds and maintains the most classified, scored Australian B2B business dataset available as a direct download.

Our data is sourced from the Australian Business Register (ABR) public dataset, licenced under CC BY 2.5 AU. Every record is cleaned, postcode-matched to suburb and LGA, industry-classified using ANZSIC codes, and scored across 8 buying propensity dimensions.

We're a Perth-based data business. ABN 13 002 879 686. Questions or custom data requests: hello@prospectsignal.com.au

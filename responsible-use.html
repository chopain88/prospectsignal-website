Responsible use

Use the data like a grown-up.

Prospect Signal AU products are licensed for responsible B2B research and planning. They are not permission slips for spam.

Licence terms

Standard packs are licensed for single-organisation internal use. Your team may import the file into internal CRM, sales, analytics and planning systems for that organisation.

What buyers can do

  • Use the data for B2B market research, territory planning and account prioritisation.
  • Import records into internal tools and enrich them with your own notes.
  • Use public business details as one input in a compliant outreach process.
  • Download the free sample without email capture before buying.

What buyers cannot do

  • Resell, repackage, publish or redistribute the dataset.
  • Represent the records as opted-in leads.
  • Use the data for consumer marketing or unlawful campaigns.
  • Ignore suppression, privacy, Spam Act, Do Not Call or platform obligations.

Watermark and fingerprinting

Paid datasets may include buyer-specific watermarking, file fingerprints or order metadata to detect redistribution and protect the product catalogue.

Data Provenance

Where the data comes from.

The base dataset is Geoscience Australia's Operating Mines 2024 — the authoritative federal register of confirmed operating mines. Every row starts from a real GA mine record with a real GA coordinate. No estimates. No approximations.

Parent company, operator, and legal entity data is enriched from ASX annual reports, company operations pages, state tenement registers, and official M&A announcements. Every enrichment row carries an evidence URL and a validation date.

All major acquisitions from 2022 to May 2025 have been applied — BHP/OZ Minerals, Newmont/Newcrest, Whitehaven/BHP Coal, Greatland/Telfer, Westgold/Karora, Red 5/Silver Lake, Genesis/Dacian, Thungela/Ensham, Kaiser Reef/Henty, Meeka Metals/Andy Well, Hancock Iron Ore consolidation.

12 rows carry a needs_review flag — documented, not hidden. The site, coordinates, and parent are confirmed. Legal entity requires direct state register verification.
